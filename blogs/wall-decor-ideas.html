13 Ideas for Decorating your Walls

What are the different types of Wall Decor Options available in India? Khidki Homes bring you the latest design ideas.

1. MDF CNC patterned Panels

You can choose a thick (6 mm+) MDF board base. Then add another decorative MDF layer on the top. You can opt for MDF Jali or CNC cut designs & get them painted in Duco or PU Paint. If your walls are not water leakage proof then it’s best not to use MDF and rather opt for PVC or WPC.

2. Plywood

You can opt for a 6mm plywood base and add laminate or Veneer to it. Finish and installation are very important to give a neat look to your backdrop or accent wall. You can ask your Modular Furniture Contractor to add Laminate or Veneer to the rest of the home laminates. Veneer will give an actual wooden look, it will also require polish and additional maintenance from time to time.

3. Fabric Upholstered Panels

You can opt for a 6mm plywood base and get it upholstered with the Fabric of your choice. It can be aligned with the rest of your Home furniture. You can add a thin layer of foam if you want an embossed look. This type of Wall Panelling adds a luxurious look to your space. You can also opt for a wooden frame base instead of Plywood.

4. Acoustic Wall Panels

You can use Acoustic wall panels for soundproofing a space. They are generally used in offices for avoiding sound transmission. You can opt for Acoustic Grooved wooden panels or opt for acoustic tiles.

5. Natural Wood Wall Moldings

These are the most trending Wall Decor types currently in India. Since solid wood is a bit costly, instead of covering the complete wall with wood, You can use small wooden moldings. Moldings can be arranged in various patterns.

6. PVC Wall Panels

You can use PVC Panels for water-prone areas, as PVC is completely waterproof. You can also opt for WPC Panels, they are newer versions of PVC Panels with insect resistance. PVC louvers are also commonly used wall decor in India. PVC Louvers are available in various patterns and sizes, they are commonly used in the Foyer & Living Room Accent walls. If you are prone to allergic reactions, it’s safe not to use PVC in the common areas.

7. Glass & Mirror Panels

You can opt for a mirror in your Foyer & Dining areas. It is considered good as per Vastu and makes the space look bigger. Diamond cut mirrors are commonly used in Home Interiors these days. These mirrors are available in Rose Gold, Gold & Silver finishes. It’s best to use a 6 mm plywood base & then paste differently cut mirrors.

8. Gypsum Board Panels

You can opt for 3D Gypsum Decorative Wall Panel. These are generally available in white colors. You can also choose the acoustic series in Gypsum Panels which helps in noise reduction as well.

9. Wall Paint

You can opt for different colors of Wall Paint. Choose a color different from your existing wall color. You can opt for Warm Neutrals colors to evoke a warm and welcoming emotion. Most importantly, your Home is a reflection of who you are, so choose whatever makes you feel the most comfortable.

10. Textured Paint

You can opt for a Textured Paint Wall as your Accent Wall to highlight your Living room Wall, Foyer area, Pillars, or Bedroom Wall. Textured Paint requires one or two additional material layers (like Asian Paints Royale Play) for creating a texture followed by Sanding. There could be more iterations of paint, depending upon the complexity of your texture, color, and design.

11. Wallpaper

You can opt for Wallpaper for your Home Renovation. There are endless wallpaper options available. It gives a clean finish that textured paint is unable to give.

12. Charcoal Wall Panels

You can opt for Charcoal Wall Panels, these are the 3D wall claddings available on the market. Some of these look like textured wall paint.

13. Concrete Wall Tiles

You can also opt for Seamless Concrete wall panels or Concrete Tiles to give your space a raw Industrial look. This is prevalent in luxury design & is used in Architectural Design.
