Cabinet Finishes

Acrylic

High Gloss finish with mirror-like look, available in wide variety of vibrant colours, Most Expensive Cabinet option of all

Matt Laminate

Laminates with normal finish that are easy to maintain and durable

Glossy Laminate

Ideal for small spaces, as the glossy finish reflects light and makes the space look bigger

Lacquered Glass

Lacquered glass is durable and low-maintenance, use of lacquered glass is a good choice for modular kitchen interiors

PU & Duco Paint

PU & Duco Paint both can be used to paint the raw cabinets, PU paint is more lasting and expensive choice

Membrane Finish

Budget friendly option, made of PVC. It might decolurize when exposed to sunlight and peel off over time

Veneer

Veneer sheets are made from Solid wood, these are then polished to give a rich wooden look

Kitchen Countertops

Granite

Granite countertops are the best cost-effective solutions

Quartz

Quartz is non-porous material, unlike Granite and Marble, is a bit expensive but used widely nowadays in kitchen

Marble

Marble is more porous than granite, hence requires more maintanence, and is less recommended
