Paint Finishes Types

High Gloss

Benefits of using High Gloss finish is that it is highly reflective, most durable and washable

Semi Gloss

Benefits of using Semi Gloss finish is that it is reflective and extremely durable, but not too shiny

Flat/Matte

Benefit of using Matte finish is that it is non reflective and has a disadvantage of being the least-durable

Satin

Benefit of using Satin finish is that it has pearl like appearance and is slightly reflective

Eggshell

Benefit of using Eggshell finish is that it has low lustre, velvety paint and is more durable than matte finishes
